Gliomas are the most frequent primary brain tumors
in adults, presumably originating from glial cells and
infiltrating the surrounding tissues. Despite considerable
advances in glioma research, patient diagnosis remains poor.
Using a convolutional neural network and transfer learning
BrainAI is able to use FLAIR (fluid-attenuated inversion recovery)
segmentation to identify potential tumors
